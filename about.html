Our history

Chicago captivated us with its beauty, becoming the chosen home for three Venezuelan sisters after a journey that took us far from our native country. As we settled into this magnificent city, we were struck by the fast-paced rhythm of daily life and the lack of time to cook, leading many people to rely on highly processed frozen options that contribute to health issues like obesity and heart disease. We noticed the abundance of convenient but unhealthy foods in supermarkets, motivating us to make a difference.

Inspired by our shared love for a healthy lifestyle and the authentic taste of homemade food, cooked with love by our mother, we decided to establish Healthy Scoops. Living together in an apartment in Edgewater, the eldest sister began developing recipes infused with Latin flavors, substituting some ingredients with healthier versions. The youngest sister took charge of photographing the products and creating audiovisual tools, while the middle sister managed costs and suppliers.

Our mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the frozen food industry with healthy options, blending authentic Latin flavors with modern twists. Our nutrient-packed frozen fiestas honor tradition while embracing innovation, ensuring your well-being with every delicious spoonful. We celebrate the diversity of Latin ingredients, creating an explosion of taste and nutrition in every bite, setting a new standard for healthy, convenient meals.

Join us on this journey, as we bring the love and flavors of our Venezuelan home to your table, one healthy scoop at a time.
